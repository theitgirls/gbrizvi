My biggest musical interest is the music produced using a singing synthesis program called “Vocaloid.” It stars AI characters who are attached to voice banks made through numerous samples of a real, human voice. For example, the most widely known vocaloid singer “Hatsune Miku” has the voice of Saki Fujita, a japanese actress. The program essentially takes these samples and allows producers to synthesize the character’s voice into their own melodies, ranges, and words. These characters even have real, in-person concerts using a projection on a large screen to give the illusion that they are truly there.
